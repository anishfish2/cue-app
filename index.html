Frequently asked

How does Auto mode work?
Tap Auto and Cue coaches you into position, captures the shot at the right moment, and applies a tasteful edit automatically. You can compare before/after and keep Cue's edit or the original.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Cue Pro is an auto-renewable subscription billed through your Apple Account. Manage or cancel it any time in Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

What happens to my photos?
Cue works without an account. Photos are processed only when you ask for an AI feature, sent over an encrypted connection, and are not stored on our servers. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Do I need an account?
No. Cue does not require sign-up or login.
